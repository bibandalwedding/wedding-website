OUR STORY

Once upon a time, in a land far, far away, there lived a prince who would one day travel halfway across the world in search of his true love or at least, that's how Alison likes to imagine it.

It's true that Bibek was born far away from Ireland, and in her eyes, he is Prince Charming. But that's where the fairytale endsâ€¦ and the very modern love story begins.

One dark, rainy November evening, Alison was on a bus home from Cork, scrolling through Tinder to pass the time. That's when a handsome young man appeared on her screen, his profile filled with all the right signs of being a genuine, normal human being: a college graduation photo, one with a pet, a holiday snap, and one with friends.

Naturally, she swiped right, mainly because his bio included the promise of a lifetime supply of pancakes (a claim that, it turns out, was a little optimistic).

There were no fireworks just yet, but the next day, fate stepped in when Bibek's curiosity was piqued by a quirky ice cream-loving, powerlifting, bin-bag-wearing witch (In her defence, it was Halloween). He swiped right, and after a week of chatting, they finally met in person.

They've been inseparable ever since.

Their story has taken them across the globe and on countless adventures, big and small. In April 2024, they received the keys to their forever home. By March 2025, their little family grew by four paws with the arrival of their beloved pup Busby.

Now, they're getting ready for their greatest adventure yet...marriage.
